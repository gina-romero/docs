_Learn how to export your candidate data for reporting, sharing, or backup._

<Note>
  **Note:** Only users with **permissions** to export can export candidate data.
</Note>

### How to Export Candidates

#### Option 1: Export from the Candidates Page

1. **Go to the Candidates page**
   - Navigate from the left-hand menu.
2. **Apply filters (optional)**
   - Use filters to narrow down your candidate list by attributes, tags, or segments.
3. **Select visible columns (optional)**
   - Only the columns currently displayed will be exported.
   - For example, if the **Email** column is hidden, email data will not be included in the export.
4. **Select candidates**
   - Check the box next to individual names, or
   - Use the checkbox "Name" at the top-left to select all visible candidates.
5. **Click "Export CSV"** in the top-right corner.
6. **Confirm your export**
   - Review the confirmation modal and click **Export CSV** again.
7. **Download your file**
   - You'll receive an email with a download link.
   - Click **Download CSV file** from the email.

<Note>
  **Note:** Download links expire after 14 days. If it doesn't start automatically, disable your ad blocker or copy the link into a new browser tab.
</Note>

#### Option 2: Export from a Study

1. **Open your study**
   - Go to the study that contains the participants you want to export.
2. **Navigate to the Participants tab**
   - Select the relevant sub-tab (e.g., _Shortlisted_, _Invited_, _Completed_).
3. **Filter and customize columns (optional)**
   - Apply filters to narrow your list.
   - Only visible columns will be included in your export.
4. **Select participants**
   - Check the box next to specific names, or
   - Use the checkbox "Name" at the top-left to select all visible candidates.
5. **Click "Export CSV"** in the top-right corner.
   - The file will download directly to your device.

### Troubleshooting Exporting Candidates

If your export doesn't complete or the file doesn't download, check the following:

| **Issue** | **What's Happening** | **How to Fix It** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Export file didn't download** | Browser popup or ad blocker is preventing the download. | Disable popups or ad blockers temporarily and try again. |
| **Didn't receive the export email** | The confirmation email may be delayed or filtered. | Check your spam or junk folder. If not received, repeat the export. |
| **Download link expired** | Links automatically expire after 14 days. | Re-export the data to generate a new link. |
| **Missing data in exported file** | Hidden columns weren't included in the export. | Re-export after ensuring all relevant columns are visible. |
| **Export option grayed out** | You don't have admin permissions. | Contact your account admin to request export access. |
